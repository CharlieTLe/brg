There are several models of activity or you can come up with a plan manually with some assistance.
  • Optimal assumes that everybody deploys and you have reasonable success with the missions. It is not necessarily a lower bound -- the missions are just estimates of how well you do, but it is an optimistic view. You might score a star or two higher, especially if you are close on some sections. You won't make it if participation is low.
  • Decreasing is in between. It assumes full participation and good performance on the early phases and tapers off on the later phases (this matches what I observe).
Contact @gorgatron#3094 to tailor these assumptions.


See also https://genskaar.github.io/tb_geo/html/ls.html.
